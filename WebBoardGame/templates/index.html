RULES

The game consists of cells that may include spesific actions to take on the player that stepped on it. The color of the cell indicates some information about it:

Gray->Empty
Blue->Artifact
Red->Drop Credits
Green->Jump with an Amount
Dark Green->Jump to Spesific Cell
Cyan->Draw a Card
Add->Add Credits
Orange->Penalty for Fixed # of Turns
Purple->Land Pay or Buy

This is a turn-based game. That is, the players will play when the turn is on them. Depending on the winning condition the game will finish.
